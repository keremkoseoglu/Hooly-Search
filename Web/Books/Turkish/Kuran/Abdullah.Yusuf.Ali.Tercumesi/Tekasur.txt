102/1 The mutual rivalry for piling up (the good things of thisworld) diverts you (from the more serious things),
102/2 Until ye visit the graves.
102/3 But nay, ye soon shall know (the reality).
102/4 Again, ye soon shall know!
102/5 Nay, were ye to know with certainty of mind, (ye wouldbeware!)
102/6 Ye shall certainly see Hell-Fire!
102/7 Again, ye shall see it with certainty of sight!
102/8 Then, shall ye be questioned that Day about the joy (yeindulged in!).
